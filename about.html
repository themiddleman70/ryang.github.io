Background

Although I was born in Iowa in 1995, I've since found my way to Texas, though it wasn't my first stop after leaving Iowa. I've lived in Georgia and Utah, and Texas, where I graduated from Friendswood High School in 2014. Later, I earned an Associate of Science in Computer Science from San Jacinto College in December 2016, followed by a Bachelor of Science in Applied Mathematics and a Minor in Computer Science from Texas State University in August 2019.

After completing my bachelor's degree, I set my sights on returning to Utah, a place that feels like home to me because of its breathtaking scenery and the lasting friendships I've made there. In November 2020, my dream came true when I received a job offer from SelectHealth in Salt Lake City. I lived and worked there for the next couple of years. Unfortunately, personal issues arose in July 2023, leading me to return to Texas to live with my parents.

While living with my parents, I did my best to work on my personal issues that I was dealing at the time, and while doing so, I started looking for a new career, to which I did in May 2024. As of today, I am still in my position at Integral Care.

Fun Stuff

My interest in photography blossomed during a digital photography course in high school, and since then, I've been exploring different landscapes to capture stunning shots of trees, mountains, lakes, and more.
